Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) uses software robots to automate repetitive, rule-based tasks. Think of it as a digital workforce that works 24/7, makes fewer errors, and operates at lightning speed.

  • Emulates human actions in desktop and web applications
  • Performs rule-based, repetitive tasks
  • Stable, accurate and predictable processes

Orchestrated with AI Agents

AI agents enhance RPA by adding a layer of intelligence. This means that while RPA handles repetitive tasks, AI can analyze data, make decisions, and optimize processes.

  • Process unstructured and semi-structured data
  • Agents use existing business data to inform decisions
  • Human-in-the-loop and/or straight-through processing
